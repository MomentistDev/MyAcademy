# KPI Success Metrics

## Launch KPIs (First 90 Days)

- **Org activation time:** median time from signup to first active onboarding template under 24 hours.
- **First value time:** median time from org signup to first learner completion under 48 hours.
- **Onboarding completion uplift:** at least 20% improvement versus customer pre-MyAcademy baseline.
- **Admin weekly active rate:** above 60% across onboarded organizations.
- **Trial-to-paid conversion:** target 8-12%.

## Product Health KPIs

- Course assignment to completion rate.
- Overdue onboarding instance rate.
- Quiz pass rate by template and department.
- Reminder engagement (open/click where available).
- Certificate expiry compliance completion rate.

## Commercial KPIs

- MRR growth rate month-over-month.
- Expansion rate from Free to Growth and Growth to Enterprise.
- Gross churn and net revenue retention.
- Average revenue per organization.

## Operational KPIs

- P95 API response time for core learner flows.
- Dashboard render latency for org admin/trainer views.
- Failed automation job rate.
- Support ticket resolution SLA.

## Data Collection Notes

- Collect all KPIs with tenant-level segmentation.
- Track cohort views by signup month and industry.
- Validate event data quality weekly during first three months.
