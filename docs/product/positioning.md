# Positioning: MyAcademy

## Category

MyAcademy is a **Corporate Learning Operating System** for SMEs and scaling organizations.

## Core Promise

Turn onboarding and mandatory training into a repeatable, measurable operating process instead of an ad-hoc content library.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

- SMEs with 50-300 employees.
- Startups scaling headcount quickly.
- Franchise or multi-branch businesses with distributed teams.
- Sales-led teams requiring consistent onboarding and certification.

## Buyer and User Personas

- Buyer: HR manager, operations leader, or business owner.
- Daily user: Org admin, trainer/instructor, learner.
- Economic buyer trigger: reduced onboarding time and compliance risk.

## Why We Win

- Onboarding automation is first-class, not a bolt-on.
- Multi-tenant + branch-aware structure fits growing organizations.
- Learning and performance visibility in one system.
- Subscription model with upgrade path from team to enterprise.

## Messaging Pillars

- **Automate onboarding:** trigger role-based journeys instantly.
- **Prove completion:** track progress, certifications, and due compliance.
- **Enable managers:** surface overdue and at-risk learners early.
- **Scale with control:** RBAC, audit logs, and tenant isolation by design.
