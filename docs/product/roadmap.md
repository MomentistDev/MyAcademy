# Product Roadmap

## Phase 0 (Weeks 1-3): Foundation

- Multi-tenant architecture and tenant guardrails.
- RBAC baseline and auth/session setup.
- Event infrastructure and shared UI foundations.

## Phase 1 (Weeks 4-8): LMS + Onboarding Core

- Course and content management.
- Quiz builder v1 and learning paths.
- Onboarding templates and auto-assignment triggers.
- Learner dashboard focused on daily priorities.

## Phase 2 (Weeks 9-12): Proof of Impact

- Certificate issuing with expiry support.
- Notification engine (email + in-app).
- Manager and trainer analytics views.

## Phase 3 (Weeks 13-16): Revenue Readiness

- Subscription tiers and entitlement checks.
- Billing integration and usage metering.
- Audit logs and launch hardening.

## Post-MVP Differentiation

- Skill framework and automated upskilling recommendations.
- AI assistant for content and assessments.
- SOP-to-course converter.
- Career pathing and role progression maps.
- ROI tracking tied to performance outcomes.
